# PMT & IPMT Functions for Loan Calculations

## Overview

This section focuses on using Excel’s financial functions **PMT** and **IPMT** to calculate loan payments and analyze the interest and principal components of mortgage-style loans. The goal is to understand how loan payments are structured and how Excel can be used to model real-world financial scenarios.

---

## Skills & Concepts Covered

### Loan Payment Calculations

* Understanding how loan repayments are structured
* Calculating fixed periodic payments using the **PMT** function
* Adjusting formulas for monthly and yearly loan payments

### Interest & Principal Breakdown

* Using the **IPMT** function to calculate the interest portion of a payment
* Understanding how the interest component decreases over time
* Analyzing how payments are split between interest and principal

### Financial Modeling in Excel

* Building spreadsheets for mortgage-style loan calculations
* Adjusting formulas for different payment frequencies
* Understanding the relationship between interest rate, loan period, and payment amount

---

## Practical Use Cases

* Calculating monthly mortgage payments
* Breaking down interest vs principal payments in loans
* Modeling loan repayment schedules
* Analyzing the financial impact of different interest rates
* Creating loan payment calculators in Excel

---

## Notes

This section focuses on practical understanding of **Excel financial functions** used in loan analysis rather than advanced financial modeling. Emphasis is placed on building intuition around loan payment calculations and applying Excel formulas to real-world financial scenarios such as mortgages and installment loans.
